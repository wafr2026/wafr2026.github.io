Optional full-day trip to the Arctic Circle offered to WAFR 2026 and UBISS 2026 attendees.
Our charter bus is expected to depart from Oulu around 8am and is expected to return to Oulu by 9pm.
Fee is 100 EUR.

The trip includes visits to:
- [Konttaniemi Reindeer Farm](https://porofarmi.fi) (lunch included),
- [Arktikum Science Centre & Museum](https://arktikum.fi/en/) (guided tour included),
- [Santa Claus Village & Arctic Circle & Santa Claus Office](https://santaclausvillage.info),
- [Christmas House Restaurant](https://santaclausvillage.info/restaurants/christmas-house-restaurant/) (buffet dinner).
